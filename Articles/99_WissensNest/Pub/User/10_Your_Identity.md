# WissensNest — Your Identity

## What Your Name Is For

WissensNest is shared by everyone in the family. Your name appears in the sidebar so you know whose session is active. In the future it will also be used to give you your own private space — conversations and projects that only you can see — alongside the shared content everyone accesses together.

---

## First Time on a New Browser {#first-time}

The first time you open WissensNest in a browser that has not been set up for you, a dialog appears asking who you are.

**If other family members have already registered**, you will see a list of names:

```
Who are you?

  Mama
  Papa
  Anna

  [ I'm new ]
```

Tap or click your name. The app links this browser to your identity. You will not be asked again on this browser unless you clear the browser's stored data.

**If you are the first person to open the app**, no list is shown — you go straight to the name entry step below.

---

## Entering Your Name for the First Time {#new-name}

If you clicked "I'm new", or if no family members have registered yet, a text field appears:

```
Your name
┌─────────────────┐
│                 │
└─────────────────┘
            [ Save ]
```

Type your name and press **Save** or hit **Enter**. Your name is stored in the browser and registered on the server. Other family members will see it in their list the next time they set up a new device.

---

## Changing Your Name {#rename}

Click the name badge in the top-left corner of the sidebar (next to "WissensNest"). A dialog appears with your current name pre-filled. Edit it and click **Save**, or press **Escape** to cancel without changing anything.

Renaming updates your record on the server immediately. Other devices you use will show the new name the next time they load the app.

---

## Setting Up a New Device {#new-device}

When you open the app on a phone or a different computer for the first time, follow the same steps as [First time on a new browser](#first-time) above — pick your name from the list. After that, the new device uses the same identity as your other devices.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

**What happens if I clear my browser data?**
Your name disappears from this browser's memory. The next time you open the app, it will ask who you are again. Pick your name from the list and your identity is restored — no server record is lost.

**Can two people use the same name?**
The app does not prevent it, but it will be confusing. Use distinct names so the list is unambiguous.

**Can someone else pick my name on a new device?**
Yes — there is no password. WissensNest relies on everyone in the household being trustworthy. Anyone on the home network can open the app and pick any name. This is intentional for a private home tool.

**Is my identity shared between different browsers on the same computer?**
No. Each browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) has its own separate storage. If you use two browsers on the same computer, you need to set up your identity in each one.

**Will my conversations disappear if I pick the wrong name?**
No. All conversations are still in the database. Picking a different name changes only which name the badge shows — it does not move or delete any data.
